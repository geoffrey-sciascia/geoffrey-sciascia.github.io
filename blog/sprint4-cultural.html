Research Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people Neuroplasticity is the brain's ability to modify, change, and adapt its' own structure and function throughout life and in response to experience. Studies have shown that when people understand the principles of neuroplasticity, they perform better when learning a new task and executing it. This is because when you are aware of your own neuroplasticity, your learning capacity isn't bound to the idea that we are all born with limited intelligence, but rather, through persistence and repetition, your brain can learn any piece of knowledge or task.
